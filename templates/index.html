What is Steganography?

The Art of Invisible Communication

Steganography is the practice of concealing a message within another object, such as an image, in such a way that no one, apart from the sender and intended recipient, suspects the existence of the message.

Unlike cryptography, which scrambles the message so it cannot be understood, steganography hides the message so it cannot be seen.
